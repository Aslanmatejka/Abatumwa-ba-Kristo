About Abatumwa ba Kristo

USA-based missionaries building God's kingdom in Uganda through faith, love, and service

Our Story

A journey of compassion and commitment to making a difference

Abatumwa ba Kristo (meaning "Messengers of Christ" in Kinyarwanda) was founded in 2011 by a group of passionate believers called by God to serve the people of Uganda. What started as a small missionary team has grown into a comprehensive ministry serving thousands across Ugandan communities.

Based in the United States with active mission fields throughout Uganda, we have remained committed to our calling of sharing the Gospel while meeting practical needs. Our work spans evangelism, church planting, education, healthcare, and community development, always with Christ at the center.

Today, we are blessed to work alongside dedicated missionaries, Ugandan partners, volunteers, and supporters who share our vision of transformed lives and communities through the love of Jesus Christ.
